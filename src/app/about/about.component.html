About Budget Buddy

Budget Buddy is your personal finance companion, built to help you track spending, manage income, and make smarter financial choices — all in a clean, user-friendly interface.

Why Budget Buddy?

  • Track your expenses and income by category
  • View summaries by month or year
  • Identify spending habits and trends
  • Clean, intuitive UI with helpful visuals

Our Mission

Managing your money shouldn’t feel overwhelming. Budget Buddy simplifies personal finance tracking so you can stay focused on your goals — whether that's saving, budgeting, or simply spending smarter.

Built With ❤️

This project is powered by Node.js, Express, and MySQL on the backend, with Tailwind CSS on the frontend. Budget Buddy is made for real people managing real budgets.
